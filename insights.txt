RETAIL SALES & CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION — KEY INSIGHTS
=====================================================

1. SALES GROWTH
   - Sales grew from $484K (2014) to $733K (2017)
   - 51% growth over 4 years

2. CATEGORY INSIGHTS
   - Technology is highest revenue category ($836K)
   - Furniture has lowest profit margin despite high sales
   - Office Supplies has best profit ratio

3. CUSTOMER SEGMENTS
   - Champions (64 customers) spend avg $9,479 each
   - 335 At-Risk customers need immediate attention
   - 96 Lost customers haven't bought in 559 days

4. REGIONAL INSIGHTS
   - West region leads with highest sales
   - South region needs growth strategy

5. RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Reward Champions with loyalty programs
   - Send win-back emails to At-Risk customers
   - Reduce discounts on Furniture to improve margins
   - Focus marketing budget on West & East regions
